Sport Utility Vehicle variables:

VEHICLE = the name and model of the vehicle.

MSRP = manufacture's suggested retail price IN 2000 for the base model.
			
CARGO = cargo space measured in cubic feet.
			
MPG = miles per gallon, averaged between city and highway.
			
LITERS = size of engine in liters.	
		
CYL = number of cylinders in engine.
			
HORSE = engine horse power.
